👁️Its faster than the blink of an eye, its a word from GodThis confirms the words of God Almighty, who says in his holy book The Holy Quran:{إِنَّمَا قَوْلُنَا لِشَيْءٍ إِذَا أَرَدْنَاهُ أَن نَّقُولَ لَهُ كُن فَيَكُونُ} The translation:{Indeed, Our word to a thing when We intend it is but that We say to it, Be, and it is} [<a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fdQyD8B_odY&amp;t=2m40s">2:40</a>].{وَهُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ بِالْحَقِّ ۖ وَيَوْمَ يَقُولُ كُن فَيَكُونُ ۚ قَوْلُهُ الْحَقُّ ۚ وَلَهُ الْمُلْكُ يَوْمَ يُنفَخُ فِي الصُّورِ ۚ عَالِمُ الْغَيْبِ وَالشَّهَادَةِ ۚ وَهُوَ الْحَكِيمُ الْخَبِيرُ}The translation: {And it is He who created the heavens and earth in truth. And the day He says, Be, and it is, His word is the truth. And His is the dominion [on] the Day the Horn is blown. [He is] Knower of the unseen and the witnessed; and He is the Wise, the Acquainted} [6:73].{بَدِيعُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۖ وَإِذَا قَضَىٰ أَمْرًا فَإِنَّمَا يَقُولُ لَهُ كُن فَيَكُونُ} The translation:{Originator of the heavens and the earth. When He decrees a matter, He only says to it, Be, and it is} [2:117].
